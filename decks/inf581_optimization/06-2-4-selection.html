Mating selection


Can either:

  • Randomly select parents
  • Select among an “elite” population (best ones): apply a competition

Survivor Selection

Select individuals for the next generation among:

  • Children (“offspring”) only
  • Children + parents (best individuals can live several generations)


Notation for Evolution Strategies:

$(\mu \color{\red}{+} \lambda)\text{-ES}$ selection in $\{ \text{parents} \} \cup \{ \text{offspring} \}$
$(\mu \color{\red}{,} \lambda)\text{-ES}$ selection in $\{ \text{offspring} \}$
offspring = children
